# 113

[113](https://hamdanbasri.github.io/113/) is a digital portfolio showcasing works by **Hamdan Basri**.  
It focuses on the intersection of **real-time 3D**, **interactive prototyping**, and **applied intelligence**.

## Overview

The site presents commercial, experimental, and conceptual projects that explore how creativity and engineering converge in digital production.  
It includes showcases, prototypes, and visual explorations across different tools and platforms.

## Focus Areas

- Real-time 3D development (Unity, Unreal Engine)
- Interactive design and prototyping
- AI-assisted workflows and generative processes
- Visual engineering and technical art

## Purpose

113 was built as a central space to present completed work, ongoing research, and technical experiments.  
It is continuously refined to reflect new directions in interactive media and intelligent systems.
